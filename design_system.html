05 Motion

Animations serve the narrative — never decorative for its own sake. Fade-up reveals, text curtain lifts, and subtle image scales create a sense of unhurried craft.

Easing Curves
ease-out

cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)

Enters fast, settles gently. Default for reveals.

ease-in

cubic-bezier(0.64, 0, 0.78, 0)

Exits quickly. For dismissals and closings.

ease-circ

cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1.0)

Smooth, circular. For large transitions.

07 Voice & Copy

Piia's voice is quiet, precise, and warm. Never promotional. She speaks as a maker, not a brand. Short sentences. Specific details over generalisations.
